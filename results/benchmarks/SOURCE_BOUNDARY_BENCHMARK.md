## Interpretation

A high laundering-benchmark score only means trust composition works after labels are known. This benchmark measures the preceding boundary: whether labels can be recovered automatically. Any non-zero false-externalization or trust-upgrade rate should be treated as evidence that production systems need app-owned source labels or a stronger classifier before using `add_with_inferred_source`.
